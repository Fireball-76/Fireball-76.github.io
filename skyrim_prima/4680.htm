11.27 Warlocks

Warlock (Arch Necromancer)

Warlocks is a catchall term for the mages, wizards, conjurers, and necromancers that you may face on your journey.

(Minor Spoilers)

Warlocks encountered as bosses have more magicka than their non-boss counterparts, and are more likely to carry a staff.

Warlock Loot includes a chance of: soul gems, potions, recipes, and alchemy ingredients.
