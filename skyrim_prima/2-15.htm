Crafting Stations

Note

Due to the large number of crafting stations, only one or two examples of each type of crafting station are listed for each Hold. However, individual locations within the Atlas that have stations are noted.

Alchemy Lab *

Please click to enlarge

A table-sized laboratory where you can mix Ingredients into potions and poisons by using your Alchemy Skill.

Arcane Enchanter *

Please click to enlarge

A table-sized piece of furniture where you can enchant or disenchant magical items using your Enchanting Skill.

Anvil or Blacksmith Forge *

Please click to enlarge

The tools of a Blacksmith, a Forge or Anvil allow you to forge metal, leather, and more esoteric materials into weapons, armor, and jewelry by using your Smithing Skill.

Cooking Pot and Spit

Please click to enlarge

A spit, stew-pot, or other implement where meats and ingredients can be cooked. There are a huge number of these; one in almost every house.

Grindstone *

Please click to enlarge

A foot-driven stone wheel that allows you to sharpen and improve weapons by using your Smithing Skill.

Smelter *

Please click to enlarge

A furnace that allows you to Smelt Ore (and some Dwarven materials) into Ingots for Smithing or selling.

Tanning Rack *

Please click to enlarge

A wooden rack that allows you to dry pelts and tan them into leather (or leather strips) for Smithing or selling.

Wood Chopping Block

Please click to enlarge

An old tree stump where you can split logs into firewood. They can be found in almost any lumber camp or settlement.

Workbench *

Please click to enlarge

A sturdy bench that allows you to temper and improve armor by using your Smithing Skill.
