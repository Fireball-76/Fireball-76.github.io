11.25 Vampires

Please click to enlarge

Vampirism begins as a disease, Sanguinare Vampiris, but quickly progresses to something much worse if left untreated.

(Minor Spoilers)

Vampires tend to congregate in clans, and their fearsome reputation is well deserved.

It’s worth spending those scrolls and potions you’ve been hording to defeat higher-level vampires.

You can’t let the fight drag on because their primary attack heals them while hurting you.

Vampire Loot includes a chance of: Lockpicks, Potions, Jewelry, Gems, Gold, Books, or Staffs.
