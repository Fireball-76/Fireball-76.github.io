Introduction

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Jumbo Map Pack is an interactive online map for the upcoming game from the creators of Fallout 3.

  • Overview

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Jumbo Map Pack is an interactive online map and includes:

  • The world map of Skyrim
  • All 9 Hold capital cities
  • Over 160 interior maps
  • Over 350 primary locations, 200 secondary locations and over 1,000 points of interest
  • The user can toggle points of interest on and off and zoom in and out
  • This map covers the following platforms: XBox 360, PS3, PC
