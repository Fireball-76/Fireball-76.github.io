11.23 Thalmor

The Thalmor are the agents of the elven Aldmeri Dominion, charged with overseeing the implementation of the White-Gold Concordat, the peace treaty between the Dominion and the Empire.

(Minor Spoilers)

Most view them as spies, or worse. Thalmor Loot includes a chance of gems, food, or drink.

Thalmor Wizard Loot includes a chance of gems, soul gems, potions, or ingredients.
