11.10 Dwarven Automatons

Please click to enlarge

Dwarven Automatons (Centurion)

(Minor Spoilers)

Dwarven Automatons are the mechanical constructs left behind by the Dwarves who once inhabited Skyrim.

As constructs, Automatons are immune to poison, Illusion spells, and Frost spells, and Soul Trap.

Dwarven Loot includes a chance of: arrows, scrap metal, oil, ore, gems, or soul gems. Spheres give more (and more valuable) loot than Spiders, and Centurions more than Spheres.
